Lonzo Ball is the eldest son of LaVar and Tina Ball. He is 20 years old and has two younger brothers, LaMelo and LiAngelo. Lonzo was the second overall pick in the first round of the 2017 NBA draft; he was picked by the Los Angeles Lakers. Previously, he played for the UCLA Bruins, and before that the Chino Hills Huskies.

He has his own signature shoe named the ZO2, retailing for $495.00 - $695.00 on the Big Baller Brand official website. There are multiple variations of the shoe, all for the same price. Additionally, he has his own clothing line the "ZO2 Collection". It was launched last year, and you can find it here. Along with being a big baller, Lonzo Ball is an established rapper. He released his own hip-hop album "Born2Ball" just this year on February 15, and it has an astonishing 17-song track list. Before his album he had a single called "Melo Ball 1", a song about his younger brother LaMelo.

Lonzo and his girlfriend Denise are actually pregnant with a girl they will name Zoey Tina ball, after Lonzo's mother. They have been together for three years.
