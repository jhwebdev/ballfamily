LaMelo Ball is the youngest member of the Ball family at only 16 years old. He has two older brothers in LiAngelo and Lonzo, and like them went to Chino Hills high school. In his first season at Chino Hills, LaMelo won the California Interscholastic Federation (CIF) state championship and was recognized as one of the top freshmen in the country.

Just like his older brother Lonzo, LaMelo has his own signature shoe named the Melo Ball 1. This pair of shoes retails for $395.00 - $595.00 on the Big Baller Brand official website. He is the first high school player to have a signature shoe.After Chino Hills hired a head coach that LaVar did not like, LaMelo was pulled out of the high school to be homeschooled. He later signed to play with the Vytautas Prienai–Birštonas in the LKL.

He is also in the family's reality TV show known as Ball in the Family. It airs exclusively on Facebook and is currently on it's second season. The show mainly focuses on LaVar with LaMelo and his brothers in supporting roles.
